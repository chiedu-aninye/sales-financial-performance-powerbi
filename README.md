# Financial Performance Dashboard — Power BI

A financial performance report analyzing 14 months of sales data (January 2023 – February 2024), tracking revenue against targets, identifying performance gaps, and evaluating individual sales rep contributions.

---

## 📸 Dashboard Preview

![Financial Performance Dashboard](screenshot.png)

---

## 📌 Key Findings

- The business closed 14 months with **$19M in actual revenue** against a **$19M target**, recording a total variance of **-$367K**
- Monthly targets were met in only **2 out of 14 months** — exposing a persistent gap between forecast and execution
- Sales trended upward through **September 2023** before dropping sharply in Q4, with both targets and actuals declining in tandem
- Only **4 out of 12 sales reps** exceeded their individual targets, with variances ranging from **-11.6% to +9.0%**
- YTD performance sits at **-4.4%**, signalling an unresolved deficit heading into the next cycle

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques

- **Power BI Desktop** — report design and publishing
- **DAX** — measures for YTD calculations, variance %, and dynamic KPIs
- **Data Modelling** — relationships between sales, targets, and rep dimension tables
- **Conditional Formatting** — red/green variance indicators and trend sparklines
- **Slicers & Filters** — interactive filtering by month and sales rep

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Review sales forecasting methodology — targets were consistently missed, suggesting forecasts may not reflect ground-level reality
2. Implement targeted coaching for the 8 reps who missed their targets, particularly the -11.6% outlier
3. Investigate the Q4 2023 drop — whether driven by market conditions, seasonal factors, or internal changes
